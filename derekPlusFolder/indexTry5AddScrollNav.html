About Us

Printing Plus is a full-service print shop located in Westerly, Rhode Island. Founded in 1991, Printing Plus is known for it\’s excellent custom service and attention to detail. From initial design to finished product, the guys at Printing Plus will use their wide range of knowledge of the printing and sign industries to get you what you’re looking for.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of print and media-related services, such as:

  • Graphic Design
  • Color Laser and BandW Copies
  • Wide Format Color and BandW Printing
  • Engineering Prints
  • Offset and Digital Printing
  • Signs and Vinyl Lettering
  • Booklet Making and Saddle-stitching
  • Perforating, Scoring, Numbering and Die-Cutting
  • Laminating
  • Fax Service
  • Rubber Stamps and Engraved Plaques
